GoFish

This is a basic game of go fish using a javascript AI as the opponent. The goal is to match your selected card with the oponents cards, when cards are matched they are added to your deck of collected cards. There is also an option to change the style of the deck, more decks could be added to the game easily by adding the image link and name to an array of objects.

Price Compare

This is a web application that utilizes javascript to easily compare the prices of similar items and visualizes the better deal based on price per unit of measurement. Price Compare can be used most effectively when buying products in bulk and effectivly removes the need for a calculator, automating a very simple but some what tedious and frustrating aspect of comparing prices.
